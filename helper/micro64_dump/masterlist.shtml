The Master List
The All-Encompassing Manuscript of Every Worldwide N64 Release

Welcome to the most comprehensive list of every unique N64 release in the world, the Master List. It is a complete categorization of all known N64 releases with information on release date, developer, publisher, Product Codes and plenty more. Below the list itself is a collection of statistical data based on the list, such as number of releases by category and such. We here at µ64 hope this list is useful to all our visitors in some way. A lot of work has gone into this, so enjoy it!

Key
Various terms are explained here.

NA = North America
PAL = Europe, Australia, United Kingdom, etc
JP = Japan
Product Code = The string of letters and numbers found at the bottom of an N64 cartridge label.
VRU = Voice Recognition Unit. A Microphone is connected to it for use with Hey You, Pikachu! and Densha de Go! 64.
~ = No information.
? = Missing information.

-North America and International release dates are formatted accordingly (US = MM/DD/YY)
-If a game has a North American region name, it will be given highest priority. Otherwise, the PAL or JP name is used
